Workout Routine

3-Day Split Routine

This routine is designed for intermediate to advanced lifters who are looking to build muscle and strength. It includes a mix of compound and isolation exercises to target different muscle groups.

Monday - Chest & Triceps

  • Bench Press: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Chest Flyes: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 4 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Skull Crushers: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Dips: 3 sets x failure

Tuesday - Back & Biceps

  • Pull-ups: 4 sets x failure
  • Barbell Rows: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  • Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Barbell Curls: 4 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets x 12-15 reps

Wednesday - Legs & Shoulders

  • Squats: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Military Press: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets x 15-20 reps
