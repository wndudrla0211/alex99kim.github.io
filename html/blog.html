3-Day Split Routine

This routine is designed for intermediate to advanced lifters who are looking to build muscle and strength. It includes a mix of compound and isolation exercises to target different muscle groups.

Monday - Back & Biceps

  • Pull-ups: 15 reps × 10 sets
  • Lat Pulldown: 60kg, 15 reps × 10 sets
  • Seated Row: 60kg, 15 reps × 6 sets
  • One-arm Dumbbell Row: 30kg, 15 reps × 6 sets
  • Bicep Curl: 15kg, 10 reps × 6 sets
  • Hammer Curl: 15kg, 10 reps × 6 sets

Tuesday - Chest & Triceps

  • Bench Press: 80kg, 12 reps × 6 sets
  • Dumbbell Press: 70kg, 10 reps × 6 sets
  • Push-ups: 10 reps after each set
  • Cable Pushdown: 10 reps × 8 sets
  • Overhead Triceps Extension: 10 reps × 6 sets

Wednesday - Legs & Shoulders

  • Dumbbell Squat: 10 reps × 6 sets
  • Bodyweight Squat: 10 reps × 10 sets
  • Dumbbell Lunges: 10 reps × 6 sets
  • Military Press: 15kg, 10 reps × 6 sets
  • Side Lateral Raise: 10kg, 10 reps × 6 sets
